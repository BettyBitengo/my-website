Vision for the Future

I envision rural communities where young people have the knowledge, tools, and confidence to shape their own futures. By working closely with schools and local families, I aim to support practical, hands-on learning that connects education to everyday life especially through agriculture and environmental stewardship. My goal is to help build systems that are not only sustainable, but owned and driven by the community itself, so that progress continues long after individual projects are complete.
